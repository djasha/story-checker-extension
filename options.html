How to Find Your Sheet ID

The Sheet ID is found in the URL of your Google Sheet:

  1. Open your Google Sheet in a browser
  2. Look at the URL: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID_HERE/edit#gid=0
  3. Copy the ID part and paste it in the appropriate field above

Required Spreadsheet Format

People List Sheet should have these columns:

  • 'Name' - Name of the person
  • 'Instagram URL' - Full Instagram profile URL
  • 'Facebook URL' - Full Facebook profile URL

Story Checks Log will be populated with these columns:

  • Date (Day/Month format)
  • Name
  • Platform (Instagram/Facebook)
  • URL
  • Result (YES/NO)
